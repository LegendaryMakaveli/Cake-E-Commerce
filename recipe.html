Classic Vanilla Cake Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. 2 and half cups of all-purpose flour
  2. 2 and half teaspoons of baking power
  3. Half teaspoon of salt
  4. Quater cup of unsalted butter(softened)
  5. Half teaspoon of salt
  6. 1 and half cups of granulated sugar
  7. 4 large eggs
  8. 1 cups of whole milk(or buttermilk for extra softness)
  9. 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350¬∞F (175¬∞C).Greese and flour two
    9-inch round cake pans or one 9x13 inch pan.
  2. In a medium bowl, whilsk together flour,baking powder and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, cream the butter and sugar until light and fluffy(about 2-3 minutes).
  4. Beat in the eggs one at a time,then add vanilla extract.
  5. Add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture in 3 parts,alternating with milk.
    Start and end with the dry ingredients.Mix just until smooth --don't overmix
  6. Pour the batter evenly into the prepared pan(s) and smooth the top.
  7. Bake for:
    Round pans:25-30 minutes
    9x13 pan:30-35 minutes
    or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  8. Let cool completely before frosting.

Red Velvet Dream Cake Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. 2 and quater cups of all-purpose flour
  2. 2 tablespoons of unsweetened cocoa powder
  3. 1 teaspoonof baking soda
  4. Half teaspoon of salt
  5. 1 cup of buttermilk(or 1 cup of milk + 1 tablespoon of vinegar,rest for 10 mins)
  6. 1 teaspoon of white vinegar
  7. 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  8. 1 cup ofunsalted butter(softened)
  9. 1 and half cups of granulated sugar
  10. 2 large eggs
  11. 2 tablespoonsnof red food coloring(liquid or gel)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350¬∞F (175¬∞C).Greese and flour two
    9-inch round cake pans or one 9x13 inch pan.
  2. In a medium bowl, whilsk together flour,baking powder and salt.
  3. In a small bowl, combine buttermilk,vinegar,vanilla and red food coloring.Stir well.
  4. In a large bowl, beat butter and sugar until light and fluffy(about 2-3 minutes). Add eggs one at a time,beating after each.
  5. Add the dry ingredients and the wet ingredients to the butter-sugar mix in alternating batches (starting and end with dry). Mix until just combined.
  6. Pour the batter evenly into the prepared pan(s) and smooth the top.
  7. Bake for:
    Round pans:25-30 minutes
    9x13 pan:30-35 minutes
    or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  8. Let cool completely before frosting.

Decadent Chocolate Cake Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. 1 and quater cups of all-purpose flour
  2. Quater cup of unsweetened cocoa powder
  3. 1 and half teaspoons of baking powder
  4. 1 and half teaspoons of baking soda
  5. Half teaspoon of salt
  6. 2 cups of sugar
  7. 2 large eggs
  8. 1 cups of milk
  9. Half cup of vegetable oil
  10. 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  11. 1 cup of boiling water or hot coffee(coffee enhances the chocolate flavor)

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350¬∞F (175¬∞C).Greese and flour two
    9-inch round cake pans or one 9x13 inch pan.
  2. In a large bowl,mix flour,cocoa powder,baking powder,baking soda,salt and sugar.
  3. Add eggs,milk,oil and vanilla.Mix until well combined.
  4. Carefully stir in the hot water/coffee(the batter will be thin --that's okay).
  5. Pour into prepared pan(s) and bake for:
    Round pans:30-35 minutes
    9x13 pan:35-40 minutes
    or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  6. Let cool completely before frosting.
